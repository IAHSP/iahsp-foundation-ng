The IAHSP Foundation, Inc. is dedicated to serving those in need in our communities throughout the United States and Canada through the gift and magic of ASP Home Staging. Our goal is to enrich the lives of those in need around us, whether they are individuals, organizations or institutions.

With the help of your donations we provide scholarships or sponsorships and work hard to assist those that need us to help them achieve their goals through Home Staging. We Stage® their spaces including many times painting, bringing in furniture, de-cluttering, cleaning, and literally doing all we can to improve the environment of those in need and to lift people’s spirits as well.

The IAHSP Foundation, Inc. is dependent upon your tax deductible donations and the hard work of local IAHSP chapters. Your donations can come in the form of money, or goods and services, so that we may Stage those that need Home Staging to improve their facility for the good of all who live and dwell there. This includes children’s homes, women’s shelters and a long list of organizations where ASP’s can make a real difference through Staging.

In the past we have helped many of those in need, including Ronald MacDonald Houses, Foster Care Support Foundations, The House of Ruth, The Margaret House for Women, The Children’s Home Society and individual homes.

Thank you for coming to our IAHSP Foundation Donation Site. We thank you for your donations whether large or small for together we can make a real difference and help change in the world.
